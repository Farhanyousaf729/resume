About Farhan Yousaf

MERN-stack developer and SaaS founder based in Pakistan. I build tools that solve real problems for real people.

Background

From freelance dev
to SaaS founder.

I started as a frontend developer, building UI systems and learning the full stack over time. After years of freelancing — delivering React/Next.js apps, REST APIs, and third-party integrations — I noticed a gap: GoHighLevel agencies had no good way to quickly brand client pages.

So I built GHLThemer. A drag-and-drop theme editor with 30+ niche templates, AI color generation, and conversion analytics — all deployable in 30 seconds with two lines of code. No API keys. No marketplace approval. Just results.
